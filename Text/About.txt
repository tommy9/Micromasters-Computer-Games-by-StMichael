Micromasters Computer Games

gives line-by-line listings for keying in 14 games for the Spectrum, BBC, Electron and Commodore 64. The games vary from simple blocking games to brain-teasers, games of strategy and keyboard trainers; levels of difficulty range from simple to near impossible! The listings are quick to key in, and subroutines for colour and sound are included.

Author Patricia Grady teaches computer studies and has taught mathematics from junior to diploma level. She has published books on mathematics, games and puzzles.
